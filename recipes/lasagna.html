Lasagna

A quick and easy to make lasagna. Bad looks and almost good tasting.

This is a recipe for beginners and lazy people. The finished dish is easy to eat, but not so good.

If you wish to make a proper lasagna, with goods looks and incredible taste, look elsewhere!

Ingredients

  • 10 ounces lasagna noodles
  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 03 cups cottage cheese
  • 02 eggs
  • 01 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 02 tablespoons dried parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 01 (6 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 02 (6.5 ounce) cans tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degress C)
  2. Bring a large pot of lighty salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente
  3. Drain
  4. In a skillet, brown ground beef. Using a for, mix cottage cheese, eggs, garlic powder, parsley, salt and pepper.
  5. In a separate bowl combine tomato paste and tomato sauce
  6. In a 9x13 inche baking pan, layer noodles, ground beef, tomato sauce mixture and cottage cheeshe mixture. Two layers of each will fit.
  7. Cover with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes.
  8. Remove tin foil the last 8 minutes of baking time
  9. Eat!
