Ham And Cheese Sandwich

A classic dish for the trully lazy

The easiest recipe in this website.

If you are lazy and have no cooking skills, this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • 02 loafs of bread
  • 03 slices of cheese
  • 03 slices of ham

Steps

  1. Lay one loaf of bread flat on the plate
  2. Stack the ham on top of the bread
  3. Stack the cheese on top of the ham
  4. Place the last loaf of bread on top of cheese
  5. Enjoy!
